##Zork: The Darkest Dreams

by Pau Olivé Rubio
Github Link --> https://github.com/rcpauor32/Zork_The_Darkest_Dreams/

###How to play -->

   MOVE -->
	- 'north/south/east/west/up/down'
	- 'n/s/e/w/u/d'
	- 'go' <north/south/east/west/up/down>

   LOOK -->
	- 'look' <entity_name>
	- 'look' 'player'
	- 'look' <north/south/east/west/up/down> (for the exits)
   
   OPEN/CLOSE DOORS -->
	- 'open/close' <north/south/east/west>

   PICK/DROP ITEMS -->
	- 'pick/drop' <item>

   CHECK INVENTORY -->
	- 'inventory/inv/i'
	(You just have 5 slots)

   EQUIP/UNEQUIP ITEMS -->
	- 'equip/unequip' <item>

	TIP: To equip an item it must be in your inventory.
	(for the moment has no relevance to the gameplay)

   CHECK STATS -->
	- 'stats'
	(for the moment just hp)

   PUT/GET AN ITEM INTO/FROM ANOTHER ITEM -->
	- 'put/get' <item> 'in/from' <item>

	TIP: Both items must be in your inventory to proceed.
	TIP2: Just the 'heart' and the 'skull' can contain items (limit 1 item(s) per each)
